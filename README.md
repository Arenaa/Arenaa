## About Me 🚀

I build diagnostic frameworks that expose where and why Vision-Language Models fail on fine-grained visual understanding. My research sits at the intersection of VLM hallucination, explicit reasoning, and grounded evaluation with a focus on making visual reasoning traceable and verifiable. I am actively seeking new research opportunities, so if you're interested in collaborating or discussing potential projects, please feel free to [reach out](mailto:khoshnoodi.ma@gmail.com).
